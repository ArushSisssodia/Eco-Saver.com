About Me
We are the Eco-Savers who have come with the idea of ________ which helps in reducing the land pollution
Our Prototype Model of ________ has been made using AI.
It has 2 sections:-
  • BioDegrade
  • Non-BioDegegrade
The waste which would be gone in the Bio section would get converted to compost through continous mixing and grinding. These then can be used as a manure.
The waste gone in Non-Bio Section would be first burnt using a burner. Smoke emitted would be solidifed by a solidifier and stored. Then, this waste would be stored underground and direcly sent to the factories or stations for it's recylcle.
Inspections would be there Monthly.
